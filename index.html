High-precision energy forecasts for solar power plants.

Solar energy production is unpredictable because of clouds. Sudden drops from 100MW to 15MW in minutes can cause power plants massive financial losses. Inaccurate forecasts lead to €496M in annual fines for energy traders across the EU (2024)and individual plants can face fines of up to €135K per year.

About Sunmapper

Sunmapper is a research-based project developed at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU). The startup bridges the gap between solar forecast and camera technologies research and the practical needs of the energy market. We are currently running a pilot project with European Energy, one of Europe's largest solar operators with over 3GW under management.
