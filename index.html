High-precision energy forecasts for solar power plants.

Solar energy is unpredictable because of clouds. Sudden drops from 100MW to 15MW in minutes cause massive financial losses. Inaccurate forecasts lead to €496M in annual fines for energy traders across the EU (2024). Individual plants can face fines of up to €135K per year.

Our Solution

A purpose-designed camera system that captures 4K sky images from the ground every 15 seconds.

1

Hyper-Local Resolution

10m x 10m resolution (vs. 1km+ for satellites).

2

Ultra-Short-Term

Up to 30-minute lead time forecasts served via API.

3

Precision Frequency

Data updated every 15 seconds, compared to 10-60 minutes for traditional weather models.

About Sunmapper

Sunmapper is a research-based project developed at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU). The startup bridges the gap between high-resolution atmospheric physics and the practical needs of the energy market. Industry Validation: * Currently running a pilot project with European Energy, one of Europe's largest solar operators with over 3GW under management. "Sunmapper has the potential to improve situational awareness of rapid solar power variability." — Lasse Diness Borup, Energinet. Our Mission: To provide the "missing link" in solar forecasting that satellites and numerical weather models cannot reach.
